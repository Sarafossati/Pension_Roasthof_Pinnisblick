THE VALLEY


It is not only the unique location and easy accessibility, close to Tyrol’s capital Innsbruck, that make the Stubai Valley perfect for a winter holiday.
The amazing winter sport facilities on offer at the glacier that make it ideal. Responsible for these facilities are the four ski areas with hotspots for skiers, snowboarders, freeriders and toboggan enthusiasts.
Perfectly groomed slopes, the ‘BIG Family Stubai’ special offer for all age groups, additional services for the winter season such as tobogganing, cross-country skiing, winter hiking and snowshoe hiking trails and great snow conditions guaranteed from October to June; these things are as much a given for the Stubai natives as the excellent natural food and hospitality they propose.



THE STUBAI VALLEY


The entrance of the stubai valley is well known for the two skiing- and hiking areas Schlick2000 and Serlesbahnen. Fulpmes with its rich village culture and its old tradition of blacksmithing is also worth for a visit. Due to its close distance to the Stubai Glacier, Neustift is the best-known and most visited spot of stubai valley. It is the central starting point for hiking tours to the surrounding valleys.

WINTER WONDERLAND - SKIING AND SNOWBOARDING


The Stubai valley guarantees winter fun from October till April. The four big skiing areas - Stubai Glacier, Elfer Lifte, Schlick 200 and Serlesbahnen offer countless kilometres of slopes and ski-runs. The stubai glacier is the biggest glacier skiing area of the whole region and the hotspot for skiing in the Alps. With a total of 26 cable cars and ski lifts variety is guranteed!

ENTRANCE OF THE VALLEY


At the entrance of the valley you find the villages of Schönberg, Mieders and Fulpmes. Fulpmes can look back on a centuries-old tradition of blacksmithing. A own museum and many history-charged places are indicators for that tradition.

ALPINE PASTURES AND HUTS


The valley has a variety of alpine pastures and huts. Charming and rustical places can be found in the whole valley. Numerous delicacies and specialities are offered and are worth tasting.

TO THE TOP - HIKING IN THE STUBAI VALLEY


A number of hiking tracks, like the "Stubaier Höhenweg" as well as a a network of apline pastures and huts are waiting to be visited. There are hiking tracks for all levels of skills - no matter if you are a beginner or a professional hiker!

STUBAI & TRADITION


The stubai valley can look back at a long and rich tradition. Tourism has developed the valley further in the last 100 years, but still there is a special atmosphere and charme. Far away from mass tourism the Stubai valley offers a variety of places to relax and rest. Especially the side-valleys and places at the end of the valley are worth visiting and offer pure nature!
