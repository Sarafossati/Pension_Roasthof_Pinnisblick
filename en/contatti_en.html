HOW TO FIND US


_____ BY CAR

Take the A12 motorway to Innsbruck and then change to the A13 motorway with direction Brenner/Italy. After a long bridge called "Europabrücke" take the exit Schönberg. Afterwards you have to take the Stubai Valley federal highway (B183) till you get to the village of Neder. Turn left into the "Pinnisweg" just after you pass a bus stop. Stay on the street till you reach a small chapel and just before the chapel turn left into the "Herrengasse". Afer you passed by a field you see a sawmill. Here you turn right into the "Holzgasse". Follow the street until you passed the "Cafe Marlo" and then turn right and you are at your destination. Our address is: Holzgasse 10, A-6167 Neustift im Stubaital

_____ BY A PUBLIC TRANSPORT

After your individual arrival at the main station of Innsbruck you have the possibility to take the local bus line "ST" (Stubai) of the IVB (white busses) to get to the stubai valley. The bus stop is directly in front of the main station on the right hand side (follow the signs). Get off the bus in "Neustift-Neder" after about a 50 minutes ride (30 km)
